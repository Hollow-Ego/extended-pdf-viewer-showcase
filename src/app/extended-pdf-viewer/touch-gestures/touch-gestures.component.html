By default, the pinch gesture simple zooms the entire page on mobile devices. If you want to zoom the PDF file without zooming the rest of the page, you can activate the enablePinchOnMobile option.

Note that this doesn't modify the behavior of laptops. Their touchpads translate the finger gestures to mouse wheel events. This approach works well enough, so the PDF viewer doesn't add additional support for touch gestures on laptop touchpads. In other words, enablePinchOnMobile is ignored on laptops.
